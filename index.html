Hi there, we are the Human-Centered AI-based Systems (HAIS)-Lab.

We perform research at the intersection of Natural Language Processing, Human-Computer Interaction, and Educational Technology. Specifically, we design, develop and investigate the effects of novel educational tools that enable highly individualized learning with the goal to support skill transfer and to prepare students for future learning.

We are located in the beautiful city of Bern in Switzerland and based at Bern University of Applied Sciences.

As part of our research, we have developed adaptive argumentation writing support systems that help individuals to develop better writing and reasoning skills (Link to CHI 2020 or ACL 2022 paper). Also, we built an intelligent conversational agent that provides students with adaptive and individual argumentation tutoring while writing persuasive essays independent of an instructor, time or location (Link to CHI 2021 paper). Moreover, we designed an empathy writing support tool by modeling the cognitive and emotional empathy structures in student-written texts (Link to ACL 2021 or CHI 2022 paper).

We believe in the power of collaboration for building better AI-based systems. If you are interested in our work, please contact us via Email.
